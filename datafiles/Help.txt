Thank you for purchasing my Date Picker for Android asset.

The following functions are included:

- Android_Show_Date_Picker (no return value, shows the date picker)
- Android_Get_Date_Set (returns double, 1 for date set, 0 for date not set)
- Android_Get_Day (returns string, containing day in format "00")
- Android_Get_Month (returns string, containing month in format "MMM")
- Android_Get_Year (returns string, containing year in format "1234")
